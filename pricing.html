Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our pricing and plans

Can I change plans later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and you'll receive a prorated credit or charge.

Is there a free trial?

Yes! All paid plans include a 30-day free trial. No credit card is required to start your trial.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers for Enterprise plans.

Do you offer discounts for non-profits?

Yes, we offer special pricing for registered non-profit organizations. Contact our sales team for details.

Can I cancel anytime?

Absolutely. You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. No long-term contracts.

What happens to my data if I cancel?

Your data is preserved for 30 days after cancellation. You can export all your data before canceling, and we'll delete it after the grace period.
